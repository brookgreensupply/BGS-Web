Industry & Commercial Electricity Supply

For Industrial and Commercial (I&C) customers with larger energy requirements, we can provide a broader set of electricity supply products.

Benefits include:

  • Bespoke pricing for larger consumers of electricity with variable demand profiles and seasonality
  • Expert advice on wholesale energy market pricing
  • Flexible credit solutions to help larger clients access fixed price products that may not be available through other suppliers
  • Single and multisite electricity supply contracts
  • Simple and flexible invoicing and payment options to individual sites or to a centralised billing function
  • Contracts that can be put in place over tailored periods to reflect individual customer needs and to bring portfolios of meters into alignment

As a commercial electricity supplier with direct access to the wholesale market, we will do our best to provide you with the very best electricity prices through fixed price contracts. Depending on the size of your business and consumption needs, we provide both fixed and bespoke electricity supply agreements.
