Whoa are we?

Brook Green Supply recognise the need for simple, transparent and fairly priced gas and electricity supply contracts. The UK energy markets remain unnecessarily complicated and our aim is to provide business customers with clear tariffs that they can easily understand.
Brook Green Supply Limited, a licensed gas and electricity supplier regulated by OFGEM, is part of a larger group of companies that have been trading in the energy markets since 2006 across a range of European markets including the UK, Germany and Netherlands. The Brook Green Supply management team has a combined experience in excess of 100 years in the energy markets stemming across all key business functions including trading, regulation, marketing and operations.

How do we deliver high quality energy solutions?

  • We provide both simple and more tailored products into the supply markets ensuring better commercial terms for customers
  • Brook Green Supply has an experienced Business Support Team that is responsive to individual customer requirements
  • By focusing solely on the non-domestic market and creating outstanding partnerships with consultants, we can provide better service and value to our customers
  • Our trading team works directly with generators of electricity and producers of gas to ensure that the most competitive pricing is available for customers
  • Our ongoing goal is to provide a better and more dynamic type of utility business that can adapt to a changing market and offer an alternative route to market for customers
